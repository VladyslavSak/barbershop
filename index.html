Our services

Beard mowing

The masters will tell you which shape is best for you depending on the geometry of the face and hair growth. A beard cut lasts 30 minutes and, in addition to working with scissors and a clipper, a barber will make you trim a dangerous razor.

Machine cutting

A haircut with a machine for those who are unpretentious to the length of their hair and loves simplicity and comfort. It lasts 30 minutes and the master works only with a machine with two nozzles.

Laying

We do the installation within 20-30 minutes and it is good in that you can quickly put yourself in order after work before a date. By the way, many styling smell so that they give odds even toilet water.

Haircut + Beard

Anyone who actually has a beard is recorded on a haircut with a beard. Because it is better to cultivate everything at once, rather than trying to beard separately at home, trying to save or not trusting the master. It lasts about an hour and a half and brings a lot of pleasure from the result. Recommended.

Royal shaving

Royal shaving, without exaggeration, can even be called a spa-procedure in the barbershop. Because it lasts an hour and the process takes place almost in a lying state, where you are steamed up with our branded towel with special oils several times, shaved with Italian foam.

Father + Son

We decided to please parents first of all with a special service for the future generation. A father can come with a son who is not older than 12 years old and share the pleasure together. It is very cool when father and son share values and love one place. And it’s better to instill the habit of visiting the right places since childhood.
